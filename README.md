## 🤖 Using the Generator

The interface contains clear menus for your creation process. You do not need experience in digital art or programming to build your first page.

### Creating Your First Page
1. Open the application from your desktop shortcut.
2. Select New Project from the main menu.
3. Choose a layout template for your comic page. You can pick between traditional manga styles or modern comic layouts.
4. Type a short description of the scene in the prompt box.
5. Click Generate to see your art.

### Adjusting the Results
If the image does not match your vision, you can change the settings. The software allows you to adjust the style, lighting, and detail levels. Use the sidebar tools to refine your character features or to change the background. 

## 🖼️ Managing Your Media

The software saves all your creations in a dedicated folder on your computer. You can browse your past projects from the Library tab. 

### Saving and Exporting
To keep your work, select Save from the File menu. If you want to share your comic, choose Export. You can save your images as JPG or PNG files. These formats work on social media, printing websites, and digital readers.

## 💡 Tips for Better Results

Follow these guides to get better art from the generator:

- Be specific: Instead of "a boy," try "a young boy with messy hair wearing a blue jacket."
- Use style tags: Adding terms like "ink sketch" or "colorful manga style" changes the look of your images.
- Keep the composition simple: Start with one or two characters per panel to avoid clutter.
- Check the settings: Ensure your graphics card drivers are current if the app runs slowly.

## 🛡️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

Errors are rare, but they happen. Try these steps if the app fails to start or crashes during generation.

**The app will not launch**
Check if an instance of the app is already open in your system tray. If the app remains stuck, restart your computer and try again.

**The generator stalls**
This often happens if your internet connection drops. Ensure you have a steady network. If the problem persists, clear the cache through the Settings menu.

**Graphics errors**
If the interface looks glitchy, update your graphics card software. Visit the website of your graphics card manufacturer to download the latest driver update.
